Loan Payment Calculation Steps and Requirements

  • Loan amount (A)
  • Number of periodic payments (n) = payments per year * number of years
    • Insert values into 'Number of Years in Loan' and 'Payments Per Year' boxes
    • Press 'Periodic Payments' button
  • Periodic interest rate (i) = annual rate / Payments per year
    • Insert values into 'Payments Per Year' and 'Annual Interest Rate' boxes
    • Press 'Interest Rate' button
  • Discount Factor calculation:
    • Discount Factor (D) = ((( 1 + i )^n ) - 1 ) / ( i ( 1+ i)^n)
    • Insert values into 'Number of Periodic Payments' and 'Periodic Interest Rate' boxes
    • Press 'Discount Factor' button
  • Loan Payment = Loan Amount (A) / Discount Factor (D)
    • Insert values into 'Loan Amount' and 'Discount Factor' boxes
    • Press 'Loan Payment' button
